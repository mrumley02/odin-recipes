Three-Ingredient Pasta

Description

A tasty, creamy pasta made with only 3 ingredients!

Ingredients:

  • Pasta of choice
  • Red pasta sauce
  • Cream cheese

Recipe:

  1. Boil your pasta
  2. Heat the pasta sauce and cream cheese together on the stove until the cheese has melted into the sauce
  3. Combine the pasta with the sauce
  4. (Optional) add Parmesan cheese to season
